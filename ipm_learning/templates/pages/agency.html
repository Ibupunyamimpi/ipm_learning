How it works

An app by our community of mothers — for cleaner data, richer context, and ethical AI.

Data Collections

Ibu Punya Mimpi is a web based education with community based of 70,000 Mothers. Curated by Ibu Punya Mimpi users contributors across Java, West Java, and beyond

Validation & Anonymization

Each data point will go through a dual-layer validation by trained annotators and linguists to ensure cultural accuracy, tone recognition, and language consistency. For privacy and ethical data protections, we will cover PII and made up name but still based on the context of the text.

Packaging:

Datasets tailored for fine-tuning LLMs (JSONL, CSV, speech-text) Metadata will include dialect, demographic, tone, and region, to enhance contextual fine-tuning.
